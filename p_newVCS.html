How to use this site

This tool shows you the questions that will need to be answered to implement and maintain a sustainable Virtual Care System. You probably won't be able to answer all the questions by yourself, so identify the people that have the knwodlege and skills on the different areas covered by this tool and ask them the questions. They might be people in other departments or even some of the suppliers used by your organisation.

Procuring Digital Services, Software and/or Hardware

Ask your potential suppliers these questions and assess their answers before deciding to use them:
  • Are your operations directly powered by renewable energy?
  • Do you have an (environmental) policy or charter, and if so, does it have third party certification?
  • Are transport emissions included in your energy accounting?
  • Can you provide more information about sustainability indicators beyond renewable energy, such as water usage, waste generation, and biodiversity protection?

Beware of greenwashing. Companies can say they are using renewable energy (or are 'net zero') when in fact, they are still using fossil fuels, by offsetting this use with pro-environmental investments, such as investing in renewable energy elsewhere.Find out more here and here.

Also, some scholars are concerned that the increasing use of renewable energy in the digital sector may draw this energy away from other sectors. Learn more about offsetting, and other relevant terms (such as Power Purchase Agreements), here.
